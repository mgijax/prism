Load a new J#.
Enter the J# in the text field at the top left and hit Return or tab out of the field. The first image for the J# is opened automatically.
See a given image and its panes.
Click on the figure label in the table on the left
Draw a region:
Drag the mouse over the desired section of the image.
Split regions into two.
Select the region(s) to split. Then Option-click (hold down the Option key while clicking). All selected regions will be split along a horizontal line though the clicked location. To split along the vertical, also hold down the Shift key (Option-Shift-click).
Select region(s):
Click on it. To select multiple regions, hold the shift key down while clicking.
Delete region(s):
Select the region(s) to delete and click the "Delete" button.
Associate a pane with a region:
Drag the pane label from the table and drop onto the desired region.
Mouse actions:
clickClicking on a region selects it.
shift-clickSelects multiple regions.
dragDraws a new region.
option-clickSplits all selected regions along a horizontal line through the click point.
option-shift-clickSplits all selected regions along a vertical line through the click point.
Tip: on a fresh image (no regions yet) option-click or option-shift-click on the image will automatically create a covering region and then do the split.
Keyboard shortcuts:
Note: "Option" may be "Alt" on your keyboard.
option-zUndo
option-rRedo
option-sSave
DEL or BACKSPACE Delete selected
option-x Delete selected
option-shift-xClear (delete all)
option-gGridAssign
option-oOnepane
option-cCover
option--Zoom-out
option-+Zoom-in
option-0Original size
Tip: if the app does not respond to keys being pressed, try clicking one of the buttons in the toolbar, or clicking in the J# text field. (I'm trying to find a better fix.)
